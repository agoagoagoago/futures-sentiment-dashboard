Add real social sentiment (StockTwits) as a live feed

- lib/socialSentiment.ts: keyless StockTwits fetch aggregating related tickers
  per market (CL: CL_F/USOIL; ES: ES_F/SPY/SPX). Uses native Bullish/Bearish
  tags, with a keyword-lexicon fallback for untagged messages. Per-ticker
  Promise.allSettled so one ticker failing cannot sink the rest.
- lib/scoring.ts: scoreSocialSentiment maps net bull/bear ratio to the -10..+10
  News/social sub-score, dampening small samples and setting evidence strength
  by native-tag volume.
- lib/liveMarket.ts: price + social fetched in parallel; social overwrites the
  News/social sub-score and socialContext; aggregate/bias recomputed after both.
  Graceful fallback: if social fails, sub-score keeps its stored value.
- components/SocialSentiment.tsx: bull/bear bar, counts, tickers, sample posts,
  live badge; rendered in MarketDetail; compact social line on the home cards.
- generate-report + README updated; types extended with SocialSentimentSummary.

Research only, not financial advice.
